EMERGENCY STEPS

For the injuries that require urgent first-aid, the below steps should be followed:

  • IN CASE OF BURNS:
    1. Use a fire extinguisher or smother the flames with a blanket or coat if someone is on fire.
    2. Cool the burn by running cool water over it for at least 15 minutes. Avoid using ice.
    3. Cover the burn with a clean, dry bandage or cloth.
    4. Seek immediate medical attention if it's severe.
  • IN CASE OF SEVERE BLEEDING:
    1. Apply firm, direct pressure with a clean cloth or bandage.
    2. Elevate the injured limb above the heart if possible.
    3. Call for help immediately.
  • IN CASE OF CHOKING:

  • IN CASE OF EYE INJURIES:
    1. Avoid touching the eye.
    2. Rinse the eye with clean water.
    3. Cover the eye with a cloth and seek medical attention.
  • IN CASE OF ANIMAL BITES:
    1. Clean the wound with soap and water.
    2. Control bleeding by applying pressure.
    3. Identify the animal to provide useful info to the doctor.
    4. Follow medical advice, including tetanus or rabies shots if necessary.
  • IN CASE OF DISLOCATION OR BROKEN BONES:
    1. Avoid moving the dislocated or fractured part.
    2. Consult a bone specialist immediately.
