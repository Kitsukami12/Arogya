Welcome to Arogya Sutra

Arogya Sutra is a health care support system that aims to provide natural, home-based remedies for various health conditions. We believe in the power of ancient Ayurveda and holistic healing practices that have been used for centuries in Indian culture.

Our Vision

Our vision is to empower individuals with accessible knowledge of Ayurvedic remedies while offering safety warnings for conditions that may require professional medical attention.

How We Work

Arogya Sutra combines modern technology with ancient knowledge. By leveraging AI, we aim to diagnose symptoms and provide suggestions for home remedies, focusing on those rooted in Ayurveda. However, if the symptoms match a more serious condition, our system advises users to seek immediate medical care.

Our Team

The Arogya Sutra team consists of experts in Ayurvedic medicine, modern healthcare, and AI technology. Together, we work to provide a balanced approach to health and well-being.

Core Values

  • Holistic Healing: We focus on natural and holistic health solutions.
  • Safety First: While offering remedies, we always prioritize user safety by warning against severe health risks.
  • Empathy & Compassion: Our platform is designed to assist users with care and understanding.
